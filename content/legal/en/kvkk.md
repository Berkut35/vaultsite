At Vault, we prioritize the protection of your personal data. This document outlines how we comply with data protection regulations, including the Law on the Protection of Personal Data (KVKK).

## 1. Data Controller
Vault acts as the data controller for the personal information provided during account registration and service usage.

## 2. Processed Personal Data
We process minimal personal data necessary for our services:
- **Identity & Contact:** Name, email address.
- **Transaction Security:** IP address, login records.
- **Support Data:** Any information provided during support requests.

## 3. Purposes of Processing
Your data is processed to:
- Establish and maintain your user account.
- Communicate service updates and technical support.
- Improve application performance and security.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Transfer of Personal Data
We do not sell your personal data. Data may only be shared with:
- **Service Providers:** Infrastructure providers (Supabase, Vercel) strictly for service delivery.
- **Legal Authorities:** When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

## 5. Your Rights
Under KVKK and GDPR, you have the right to:
- Learn whether your data is being processed.
- Request information on processing activities.
- Rectify incomplete or inaccurate data.
- Request erasure or destruction of your data.
- Object to processing for specific purposes.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at `privacy@vault.app`.
