At Vault, transparency is a core part of our philosophy. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on our website, and how you can manage your preferences.

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide analytical data to site owners.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We strictly separate cookies into two categories: **Essential** and **Analytics**. Because of our local-first architecture, your research data (PDFs, notes) is never tracked via cookies.

### 2.1. Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are required for the website to function correctly and cannot be switched off in our systems. They do not store personally identifiable information.

- **`vault-lang`**: Remembers your preferred language (e.g., English or Turkish) so we can serve you the correct page versions dynamically.
  - **Type:** Local Cookie
  - **Retention:** 1 Year
- **`vault-cookie-consent`**: Stores your decision on whether you accepted or declined non-essential cookies.
  - **Type:** Local Storage
  - **Retention:** Persistent

### 2.2. Analytics and Performance Cookies (Optional)

We use privacy-friendly analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our page (e.g., measuring load times and traffic volume) to improve our application. These cookies are only activated if you click "Accept All" on our Cookie Banner.

- **Vercel Analytics & Speed Insights**: These tools log anonymous interactions, counting visits and traffic sources without directly tracking your personal identity. 
  - **Type:** Third-Party (Vercel)
  - **Retention:** Session / 1 Year

## 3. Third-Party Connections

Vault relies on **Supabase** for secure user authentication globally. If you log into the Vault dashboard, Supabase sets secure HTTP-only cookies (`sb-accesstoken` and `sb-refreshtoken`) to maintain your session securely. These are classified as strictly necessary for registered users.

## 4. Managing Your Cookies

You have full control over your cookies. You can revoke your analytical cookie consent by clearing your browser cache and local storage. Most modern browsers also allow you to block cookies entirely within their security settings.

If you have any questions regarding how we respect your privacy through cookies, you can reach out to us at `privacy@vault.app`.
